Hi, I'm VaibhavI'm a self-taught front-end developer based in Mumbai, driven by curiosity and a hands-on approach to learning. My work centers around building clean, modular interfaces that are both visually intuitive and technically sound.

I specialize in HTML, CSS, Tailwind CSS, Bootstrap, and JavaScript—tools that allow me to craft responsive layouts and scalable UI components. I enjoy experimenting with design systems and utility-first frameworks to create interfaces that feel purposeful and adaptable.

My journey has been shaped by iteration and real-world problem solving. Whether it's refining a layout, optimizing performance, or troubleshooting a stubborn bug, I approach each challenge with a mindset of continuous improvement. I believe that every project—no matter how small—is an opportunity to learn, simplify, and build trust through clarity.

Beyond code, I explore visual storytelling through flyer mockups, cartoon-style icons, and locally resonant design experiments. These creative exercises help me think modularly and communicate ideas with warmth and precision.
